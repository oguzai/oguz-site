Who I Am

Attorney,
Builder, Entrepreneur.

Dorukhan Oguz — oguz.ai

I believe the most powerful thing a lawyer can do today is build — not just argue.

I'm Dorukhan Oguz, an attorney and entrepreneur working at the intersection of law and technology. My work is driven by a single conviction: legal systems are too complex, too slow, and too expensive for the people who need them most. So I set out to change that.

I founded Oguz Law to bring a modern, client-first approach to immigration and business law. From there, I built WeVisa AI — an AI-powered platform that makes immigration guidance faster and more accessible — and acquired ITIN.com to streamline the tax identification process for non-residents navigating the U.S. system.

My goal with this site is simple: to share what I'm learning, building, and thinking about as I grow these ventures — and to connect with people who care about the future of law, immigration, and technology.
